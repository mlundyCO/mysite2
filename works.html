How it works

Online tutoring allows you to get the help you need at the times that are convenient and prices that are affordable. Start by reaching out to me to discuss your needs and schedule a time to meet. Using technology, we meet in a virtual space where we can communicate using voice or text, and write on a shared, virtual whiteboard to go over your problems from the finest details, or just the broad strokes. To get started, learn more about payment, or contact me.

Technology

Communication

I use the popular tools Skype and Google Hangouts to communicate with students. We can also chat over the phone if you prefer.

Whiteboard

The simple, never-ending, automatically saving whiteboards at WhiteboardFox allow us to easily write and share complicated mathematical expressions with each other. Before our lesson, I will send you a link to the specific whiteboard that we will use for that lesson. See an example of a some math on the whiteboard here (not mobile friendly). Feel free to create some whiteboards and try it out!

Pricing and Payment
